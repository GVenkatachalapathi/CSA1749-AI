Explore Kerala - God's Own Country

Munnar

Munnar is famous for its tea gardens, lush green hills, and cool climate.

Alleppey

Alleppey is known for its backwaters and houseboat cruises, offering a tranquil experience.

Kovalam

Kovalam is popular for its crescent-shaped beaches and relaxing atmosphere.

Thekkady

Thekkady is home to Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, famous for its rich biodiversity.

Wayanad

Wayanad offers misty hills, waterfalls, and wildlife, making it a nature lover's paradise.
