Explore Bangalore - The Garden City

Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Lalbagh is a famous botanical garden known for its beautiful flora, including rare plants and flowers.

Cubbon Park

Spanning over 300 acres, Cubbon Park is a green haven in the heart of Bangalore, ideal for walks and picnics.

Bangalore Palace

Bangalore Palace is an iconic structure that showcases stunning architecture and historical artifacts.

ISKCON Temple

The ISKCON Temple in Bangalore is a spiritual and architectural marvel, offering a peaceful retreat.

Bannerghatta National Park

This national park is home to a variety of wildlife, including tigers, lions, and elephants. It also has a butterfly park.
